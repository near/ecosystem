Nearblocks.io is a Near Protocol explorer supported by the NEAR Protocol team and received Grants from Near Foundation. We provide Blockchain Explorer, Search, API, Analytics Platform for Near Protocol, and a decentralized smart contracts platform. Built and launched in early 2022, to provide a User-friendly experience for blockchain data.
